How two simple exercises changed my life

Princess Tirelo Molotlegi is a real princess who works with the National Basketball Association to expand a successful after-school NBA basketball/mentoring program in Phokeng, South Africa. For two to three hours a day, five days a week, three retired NBA players mentor a dozen youth, giving them one-on-one attention as well as group training through basketball. The program teaches essential life lessons such as the importance of teamwork, confidence, self esteem, motivation and exercise. Princess Tirelo admits that the kids took such a liking to basketball since the big sports in that region are rugby and soccer. She believes one major factor in the interest and success of the program is the attention, support and love each student receives from the coaches. Princess Tirelo is passionate about inspiring the youth and helping them discover their authentic selves. The NBA program led by her brother, King Kgosi Leruo Molotlegi, has changed the lives of students in remarkable ways. Princess Tirelo and her family have big plans on expanding the program and getting more NBA involvement with the goal of engaging, educating and inspiring these future leaders to discover what makes them authentically special. To help them grow, one can donate to support education in Africa through Thuto Thebe Educational Trust, volunteer their program design or business development skills, or become a strategic partner.
